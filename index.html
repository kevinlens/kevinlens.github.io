About Me

Hi there, my name is Kevin and I am a full-stack developer with 4 years of experience. I consider myself to be a systems thinker, meaning I don't identify as a particular frontend/backend engineer. I am more interested in solving problems holistically. Being resourceful is very important to me as this helps me achieve a “get things done” mentality no matter what the circumstance may be. If I don't don't understand something I am very quick determine what to do to push forward.


I would consider myself somewhat of a bookworm person, most likely due to my enthusiasm for epic fantasy novels like the Starlight Archive to mystery books like the Sherlock Holmes Novels. I am also very fascinated by philosophy and the idea of stoicism. Other hobbies may include playing the violin, light gaming, mentoring, and meeting new people.
